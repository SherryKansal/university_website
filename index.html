Courses We Offer

Whether you are embarking on your undergraduate journey, seeking to advance your career through graduate studies, or pursuing lifelong learning opportunities, Eduford is here to help you succeed. Join us and become part of a community that is shaping the future through education, research, and service.

B.Tech.

Our B.Tech. program offers a unique blend of rigorous academics, hands-on learning, and industry exposure, preparing students to thrive in the dynamic world of engineering. Whether you aspire to be a mechanical engineer, computer scientist, or civil engineer, Eduford provides the platform for you to realize your potential and make a meaningful impact on the world.

M.Tech.

Elevate your expertise and advance your career with an M.Tech. degree from Eduford, a premier destination for advanced engineering education. Our M.Tech. program offers specialized knowledge, cutting-edge research opportunities, and industry connections that empower students to become leaders in their fields.

BBA

Unlock your potential in the business world with a BBA degree from Eduford. Our program equips students with essential business skills, leadership qualities, and experience to excel in today's competitive environment. With expert faculty and real-world projects, Eduford provides the perfect platform for aspiring business leaders to thrive.

Our Facilities

Experience our top-tier facilities, including a world-class library for academic exploration, a spacious basketball court for athletic pursuits,
and a vibrant cafeteria offering delicious and healthy dining options. Whether you're hitting the books, shooting hoops, or enjoying a meal with friends,
our campus amenities cater to every aspect of your university experience.

World Class Library

Our world-class library is a haven for scholars. With its vast collection of meticulously curated resources spanning myriad subjects, our library offers an unparalleled academic experience. From rare manuscripts to cutting-edge journals, students have access to an extensive array of materials to support their intellectual pursuits.

Largest Playground

Our expansive basketball court provides ample space for players to showcase their skills. With regulation-sized dimensions and top-notch amenities, it's the ultimate destination for both casual pickup games and competitive tournaments. Our court offers an unbeatable experience for basketball enthusiasts of all levels.

Tasty and Healthy Food

Indulge in our cafeteria's delicious and nutritious offerings, where taste meets health in every bite. From vibrant salads with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to hearty grain bowls, our menu caters to diverse palates. Savor flavorful wraps, wholesome sandwiches, and soups made from wholesome ingredients.
